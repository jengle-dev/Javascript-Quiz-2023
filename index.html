This is a quiz to test your knowledge of JavaScript.

The quiz will have 10 multiple choice questions. Select the answer by clicking the corresponding button.

  • The next Question will appear.
  • If the answer selected is wrong, 20 seconds will be deducted from the total time.
  • The quiz is over when all 10 questions are answered or the timer has countdown to 0.
Click the Start Button to view the first question & start the timer.
